KangJung does not sell personal or sensitive user data. Any data handled by third-party services is subject to the privacy policies of those providers, including:

Data stored in the Application is used to provide the features you use, such as displaying your records, calculating dates or statistics, showing reminders, saving settings, restoring backups, processing purchases, showing ads where applicable, improving stability, and maintaining app functionality.

KangJung does not share your locally stored app data with KangJung's own server. Information may be shared only in the following limited cases:

Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete it inside the Application, clear the Application's data, uninstall the Application, or remove exported backup files yourself.
