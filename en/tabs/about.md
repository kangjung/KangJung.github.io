Hi, I'm **KangJung** — someone who keeps making things.

I've been working as a **web developer since February 2017.** The games, apps, and tools
here are **personal hobby projects**, separate from my day job. I mostly make **simple little
things I just feel like trying out** — less about polish, more about getting something made
and out there.

## What I make

- **Games** — casual & puzzle games in Unity and HTML5 (ZigZag RABBIT, Planet Game, 1010!, and more)
- **Apps** — small, practical Android apps like Emotion Calendar, D-Day, and quick-memo tools
- **Web tools** — things that run right in the browser (background remover, DevBox, etc.)

I'm also really into **pixel art** these days, poking at it bit by bit.

I join **js13kGames** every year and write up what I learn at dev conferences and events
on my [blog]({{ site.baseurl }}/en/tabs/blog/).
